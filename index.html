about us

At Sahara coach, we design and build buses of all types to meet the market demands at pace with technological advancements. Established in 2015 and originating from Hubballi, Karnataka, Sahara Coach is one of the leading commercial vehicle builder in the region. Operating from North Karnataka, our comprehensive stock holding of more than 1000 design concepts ensure that we are able to provide our worldwide customer base with the optimum level of service.
